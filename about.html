About:

At 17 while riding a four-wheeler I was involved in a bad accident. I crushed the bones in my arm and required surgery. I was fascinated with the x-rays and really liked seeing the internal healing process. I graduated high school and attended radiology school. It was hard work, especially Radiation Physics but I did it and have been working in the field for 12 years. I work for Orthopedics which is my favorite and I highly doubt I will go to a different modality. It is incredibly physically demanding but satisfying when you watch a patient from not being able to stand to then walking. My favorite things to do are to hang out with my kids, husband, and dog. I'm so blessed with my life and continue to be thankful for everything.
